# Architectural Keywords

Use these specialized terms to get better architectural results.

## Architectural Styles

### Historical
- `Classical` - Greek/Roman columns, symmetry
- `Gothic` - Pointed arches, flying buttresses
- `Baroque` - Ornate, dramatic, curved forms
- `Victorian` - Ornate trim, steep roofs, towers
- `Art Nouveau` - Organic curves, nature motifs
- `Art Deco` - Geometric, glamorous, streamlined

### Modern
- `Modernist` - Form follows function, clean lines
- `International Style` - Glass/steel, minimal ornament
- `Brutalist` - Raw concrete, massive forms
- `Minimalist` - Extreme simplicity, essential elements
- `Postmodern` - Playful, colorful, ironic
- `Deconstructivist` - Fragmented, unconventional forms

### Regional
- `Mediterranean` - White walls, terracotta, courtyards
- `Japanese` - Natural materials, simplicity, zen
- `Scandinavian` - Light wood, white, functional
- `Tropical` - Open ventilation, natural materials
- `Desert Contemporary` - Earth tones, indoor-outdoor
- `Colonial` - Symmetrical, shutters, porches

## Structural Elements

### Roof Types
- `Flat roof`
- `Pitched roof` / `Gable roof`
- `Hip roof`
- `Mansard roof`
- `Gambrel roof`
- `Curved roof` / `Vaulted roof`
- `Butterfly roof`
- `Sawtooth roof` (industrial)

### Facade Elements
- `Curtain wall` - Glass facade system
- `Rain screen` - Ventilated cladding
- `Brise-soleil` - Sun shading devices
- `Clerestory windows` - High windows
- `Punched windows` - Individual openings
- `Ribbon windows` - Horizontal strips
- `Corner windows` - Wrap-around glass
- `Storefront glazing` - Floor-to-ceiling

### Structural Systems
- `Post and beam` - Columns and horizontal beams
- `Cantilever` - Projecting without support
- `Space frame` - 3D truss structure
- `Shell structure` - Thin curved surface
- `Tensile structure` - Fabric/cable tension
- `Exoskeleton` - External structure

## Materials

### Traditional
- `Natural stone` - Granite, limestone, marble
- `Brick` - Clay fired masonry
- `Timber` / `Wood` - Framing or cladding
- `Stucco` - Plaster exterior finish
- `Terracotta` - Fired clay elements
- `Slate` - Stone roofing/tiles

### Modern
- `Reinforced concrete` - Cast-in-place or precast
- `Structural steel` - Steel frame construction
- `Glass` - Curtain walls, windows
- `Aluminum` - Panels, frames, cladding
- `Composite panels` - ACM, fiber cement
- `Cross-laminated timber` (CLT) - Mass timber

### Sustainable
- `Rammed earth` - Compressed soil walls
- `Living wall` / `Green wall` - Vegetated facade
- `Reclaimed wood` - Recycled timber
- `Recycled steel` - Reused structural steel
- `Bamboo` - Rapidly renewable material
- `Cork` - Sustainable insulation/cladding

## Photography Terms

### Shots
- `Hero shot` - Main dramatic view
- `Contextual view` - Building in surroundings
- `Detail shot` - Close-up of material/joinery
- `Interior-exterior` - Connection of spaces
- `Axonometric` - 3D exploded view

### Equipment
- `Wide-angle lens`
- `Tilt-shift lens` - Corrects perspective
- `Drone photography` - Aerial views
- `Golden hour` - Warm sunset light
- `Blue hour` - Twilight photography
- `Long exposure` - Light trails, smooth water

## Tips

1. **Use 3-5 keywords** per category (style, materials, lighting)
2. **Combine old and new**: "Traditional craftsmanship meets modern technology"
3. **Specify scale**: "Human-scale" vs "Monumental"
4. **Add context**: "Urban infill" or "Rural retreat"
